PM Compass

Place yourself on the User–Tech–Business triangle and discover your primary and secondary PM orientations.

PM Compass is a short, 12‑scenario survey that helps you see which of six product management orientations you lean toward in practice: Advocate, Innovator, Technologist, Architect, Strategist, and Operator. It’s inspired by the framing in Six Flavors of Product Management on Sociotechnical.

For each A/B prompt, choose where you land on a 5‑point scale from Definitely (left) to Definitely (right). You can go back and forth; your picks are preserved, and changing an answer updates your score. At the end, you’ll see a breakdown by orientation and a point plotted inside a colorful User–Tech–Business triangle.
